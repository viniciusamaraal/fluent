Getting Started

Welcome to Fluent Bot! Here's everything you need to know to get started.

Quick Start Guide

  1. Install the app to your Slack workspace
  2. Configure your preferences using /fluent-config
  3. Start using the bot with /fluent or /fluent-translate

Available Commands

/fluent <text>

Main command for AI chat and translation. Just type naturally and Fluent Bot will understand your intent.

Examples:

  • "Translate this to Spanish: Hello"
  • "What's the weather like?"
  • "Help me write an email"
/fluent-translate <text>

Direct translation to your configured primary language.

Example:

  • /fluent-translate Hello, how are you?
/fluent-config

Open the configuration modal to set your language preferences and AI provider.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my preferred language?

Use /fluent-config and set your primary languages in the configuration modal. You can specify multiple languages.

What AI providers are supported?

Fluent Bot supports:

  • Anthropic Claude (Sonnet 4.5, Haiku 3.5)
  • OpenAI GPT (GPT-4o, GPT-4o-mini)

You can switch providers in /fluent-config.

Are there any rate limits?

Yes, Fluent Bot has rate limits to ensure fair usage and maintain service quality for all users. If you need higher limits, paid plans will be available soon.

How much does it cost?

Fluent Bot is currently 100% free to use! We plan to offer optional paid plans with higher rate limits and additional features in the future.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We take security seriously:

  • All data encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Hosted on Microsoft Azure with enterprise-grade security
  • Minimal data collection - only what's needed to provide the service

See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Why am I getting rate limited?

Rate limits help us maintain service quality and manage costs for all users. If you're frequently hitting limits, please contact support. We're planning to introduce paid plans with higher limits soon.

Can I use Fluent Bot in private channels or DMs?

Yes! Fluent Bot works in:

  • Public channels
  • Private channels
  • Direct messages (DMs)

Just use the slash commands as you normally would.

Commands aren't working

Try these steps:

  1. Make sure you're using the correct command format: /fluent, /fluent-translate, or /fluent-config
  2. Check if the app is installed in your workspace (ask your Slack admin)
  3. Try removing and re-adding the app

Translations are incorrect

AI translations are usually accurate but not perfect. If you're getting poor results:

  • Try being more specific in your request
  • Switch AI providers in /fluent-config
  • Provide more context in your message
