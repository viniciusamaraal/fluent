Privacy Policy

How Fluent Bot collects, uses, and protects your data

Last Updated: January 19, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Fluent Bot ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Slack application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information from Slack

When you use Fluent Bot, we collect:

  • User Information: Slack user ID, team ID, email address (if provided by Slack), and display name
  • Message Content: The text you send to Fluent Bot via slash commands for translation and AI chat purposes
  • Usage Data: Commands used, timestamps, and rate limiting information

1.2 User Preferences

We store your configuration preferences including:

  • Primary language preferences
  • Preferred AI provider (Anthropic Claude or OpenAI GPT)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide Services: Process translation requests and AI chat interactions
  • Personalization: Remember your language preferences and AI provider settings
  • Rate Limiting: Enforce fair usage limits to maintain service quality
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve functionality (aggregated data only)
  • Support: Respond to support requests and troubleshoot issues

3. Data Processing with AI Providers

Important: Third-Party AI Processing

When you use Fluent Bot, your messages are sent to AI providers (Anthropic or OpenAI) for processing using our API keys.

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these providers:

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in our database hosted on Microsoft Azure with:

  • Encryption at rest
  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Regular automated backups
  • Access controls and monitoring

4.2 Data Retention

  • User Data: Retained while your Slack workspace has the app installed
  • Message Content: Not permanently stored; only processed in real-time
  • Preferences: Retained for the duration of your account
  • Deleted Accounts: All data is permanently deleted within 30 days of app removal

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share data:

5.1 With Your Consent

When you explicitly authorize data sharing

5.2 Service Providers

  • Anthropic/OpenAI: For AI processing (based on your message content)
  • Microsoft Azure: For cloud hosting infrastructure

For a complete list of our sub-processors and details about how they process data, see our Sub-Processors page.

5.3 Legal Requirements

When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: Request a copy of your stored data
  • Update Information: Modify your preferences via /fluent-config
  • Delete Your Data: Remove the app from your Slack workspace to delete all data
  • Opt-Out: Stop using the service at any time
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at: support@fluent-bot.com

7. Cookies and Tracking

Fluent Bot does not use cookies or tracking technologies. All interactions occur within Slack's platform.

8. Children's Privacy

Fluent Bot is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of Fluent Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact us:

  • Email: k19tech@gmail.com
  • Support Page: https://viniciusamaraal.github.io/fluent/support.html

GDPR & CCPA Compliance

We are committed to complying with GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California) regulations. Users in these jurisdictions have additional rights including the right to deletion, access, and data portability. Contact us to exercise these rights.
